About me

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence and Advanced Visual Computing at École Polytechnique. My coursework includes studies in advanced deep learning, computer vision, reinforcement learning, and their applications in visual computing, such as 3D computer graphics, virtual and augmented reality, generative AI, autonomous driving and robotics.

Prior to this, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Mathematical and Computing Sciences for Artificial Intelligence at Bocconi University. This program provided me with a rigorous foundation in mathematics and computer science, along with additional training in applied sciences such as physics, neuroscience and economics.

My Interests

I have a broad range of interests in AI. They are listed below in no particular order of preference—this is simply a collection of areas I am passionate about. Please, note that the list is dynamic and constantly evolving!

If any of these topics (or related fields) resonate with you, feel free to reach out to me!

  • GenAI & Multimodality

    How can we accurately sample from an unknown distribution to generate new images, videos, or 3D objects with AI? What role does multimodality play in improving these capabilities?

  • 3D Computer Vision

    We conquered 2D. What does computer vision need to conquer the 3D world? How can it connect the dots between digital representations and the real world around us?

  • Virtual & Augmented Reality

    3D interactions are the future. How far can AI go in transforming VR and AR? Can it make 3D interactions feel seamless and bring virtual worlds closer to reality?

  • AI for Science

    How can we make use of AI for scientific discovery? What architectures are needed to solve really complex physical problems?

  • Interpretability & Alignment

    What is really going on inside neural networks? Is there a way to understand them well enough to make them safer and more reliable?

  • Computational Photography

    How can AI make photos look better, both when we take them and when we edit them? Could it even redefine what photography means?

  • Autonomous Driving

    What will it take for AI to create fully self-driving cars? How does it handle perception and decision-making in chaotic real-world situations?
